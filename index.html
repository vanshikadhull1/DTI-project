How It Works

CampusSwap offers multiple ways to get what you need and share what you don't - all within your campus community.

Buy

Find affordable items from fellow students. Textbooks, furniture, electronics and more!

Sell

Turn unused items into cash. Quick listings and safe campus transactions.

Rent

Temporarily use high-value items without the full cost of buying them outright.

Exchange

Trade items without cash. Swap books, clothes, and more with your campus community.
